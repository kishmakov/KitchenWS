Kitchen IDE Terms of Use

Our Mission

Goal of the Kitchen IDE project is to provide every person in the world with the most convenient access to computational knowledge of public domain in order to flourish subsequent development, research and understanding.

Data Policy

Irreversibility

We believe that free access to the knowledge is the public benefit.

Kitchen Team will do it best to preserve all the accumulated public data. That means that every single datum of information donated to our system or generated within it will be available to all people in the world as long as our database backups would be available in the internet either in centralized or decentralized form.

Independence

We believe that there is no chosen state, civilization or culture.

Understanding that computational knowledge could bring power, we intentionally try to distribute our backups worldwide in such a manner, that no particular group of people, state or states alliance could control access to knowledge base for the rest of the world.
