Kitchen IDE Terms of Use

Our Mission

Kitchen IDE project is developed to provide every person in the world the most convenient access to computational knowledge of the public domain in order to flourish subsequent development, research and understanding.

Data Policy

Irreversibility
We believe that free access to computational knowledge is the public benefit


We will do our best to preserve all accumulated public data. That means that every single datum of information donated to our system or generated within it will be available to everybody as long as our services are on. And even more: we would update regularly decentralized version of backup for it be stored as torrents.

Independence
We believe that there is no chosen state, civilization or culture


Understanding that computational knowledge comes hand in hand with power, we are deliberately apolitical. That means that we are not just focused on providing uniform access to our services for all members of the international community, but also locate our infrastructure in such a manner, that no particular group of people, state or states alliance could shut down our services.
